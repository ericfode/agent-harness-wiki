# SkillX

## Overview
SkillX builds a hierarchical skill knowledge base from trajectories, spanning strategic plans, functional skills, and atomic skills. It is a library architecture for agents that want reusable capability across levels of abstraction.

## Why it matters
It matters because workflow evolution often needs a second accumulation surface below the graph. SkillX makes that lower layer explicit and structured.

## Distinctive trait
Its distinctive trait is hierarchical skill organization: reusable behavior is stored across strategic, functional, and atomic levels rather than in one flat bucket.
