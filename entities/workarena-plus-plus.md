# WorkArena++

## Overview
WorkArena++ extends WorkArena toward more compositional planning, reasoning-heavy, and workflow-like enterprise tasks. It also provides mechanisms for generating ground-truth traces that can support later fine-tuning or training.

## Why it matters
It matters because it moves the enterprise browser world closer to an actual curriculum for improving agents rather than a one-off evaluation sheet.

## Distinctive trait
Its distinctive trait is compositionality: tasks demand planning and reasoning across richer workflow structures than the earlier WorkArena set.
