# AgentEvolver

## Overview
AgentEvolver is a broader self-evolving-agent framework organized around self-questioning, self-navigation, and self-attribution. It is less about one workflow language than about continual autonomous improvement as a systems pattern.

## Why it matters
It matters because it shows that self-evolving agents are becoming their own literature rather than a decorative slogan attached to workflow generation.

## Distinctive trait
Its distinctive trait is introspective control: the agent improves by generating its own exploratory tasks, reusing navigation experience, and attributing success or failure to parts of its own behavior.
