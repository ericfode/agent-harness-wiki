# Trace2Skill

## Overview
Trace2Skill distills trajectory-local lessons into unified transferable skills by aggregating broad execution experience before consolidation. It is explicitly about turning many narrow traces into fewer reusable procedures.

## Why it matters
It matters because long-lived agents do not need infinitely many tiny lessons. They need a principled way to compress local experience into portable capability.

## Distinctive trait
Its distinctive trait is skill distillation from distributed trace evidence, which tries to turn brittle local patches into general reusable artifacts.
