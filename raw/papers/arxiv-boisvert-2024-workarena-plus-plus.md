# WorkArena++: Towards Compositional Planning and Reasoning-based Common Knowledge Work Tasks

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.05291)
**Authors:** Léo Boisvert, Megh Thakkar, Maxime Gasse, Massimo Caccia, Thibault Le Sellier De Chezelles, Quentin Cappart, Nicolas Chapados, Alexandre Lacoste
**Date:** 2024-07-07
**Primary category:** cs.AI
**All categories:** cs.AI

## Abstract
WorkArena++ increases the compositional planning and reasoning load of enterprise web tasks and provides trace generation for training or fine-tuning. It is closer to a real curriculum for work agents than a single-shot website toy benchmark.
