# Webstrates

**Source:** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1145/2807442.2807446)
**Authors:** Clemens N. Klokmose, James R. Eagan, Siemen Baader, Wendy Mackay, Michel Beaudouin-Lafon
**Date:** 2015

## Core idea
Webstrates turns shared web documents into live substrates that act simultaneously as applications and collaborative state containers.

## Harness takeaway
Dashboards, inspectors, and shared operational canvases can be live synchronized documents backed by one substrate rather than brittle exported views.
