# BlockA2A: Towards Secure and Verifiable Agent-to-Agent Interoperability

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.01332)
**Authors:** Zhenhua Zou, Zhuotao Liu, Lepeng Zhao, Qiuyang Zhan
**Date:** 2025-08-02
**Primary category:** cs.CR
**All categories:** cs.CR, cs.AI

## Abstract
BlockA2A treats secure interoperability as part of the coordination substrate rather than an external wrapper. The framework combines decentralized identifiers, ledger-backed auditability, smart-contract policy enforcement, and a defense orchestration layer that can flag Byzantine agents and revoke permissions at runtime. For this wiki, the paper matters because it makes trust, accountability, and revocation explicit protocol concerns inside multi-agent coordination.
