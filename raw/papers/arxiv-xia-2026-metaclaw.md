# MetaClaw: Just Talk -- An Agent That Meta-Learns and Evolves in the Wild

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.17187)
**Authors:** Peng Xia, Jianwen Chen, Xinyu Yang, Haoqin Tu, Jiaqi Liu, Kaiwen Xiong, Siwei Han, Shi Qiu, Haonian Ji, Yuyin Zhou, Zeyu Zheng, Cihang Xie, Huaxiu Yao
**Date:** 2026-03-17
**Primary category:** cs.AI
**All categories:** cs.AI

## Abstract
MetaClaw studies continual evolution for a deployed multi-channel agent platform that must improve without downtime. It combines fast skill synthesis from failures with opportunistic policy optimization during inactive windows, which is unusually concrete about the operational side of continual evolution.
