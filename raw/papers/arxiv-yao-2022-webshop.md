# WebShop: Towards Scalable Real-World Web Interaction with Grounded Language Agents

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2207.01206)
**Authors:** Shunyu Yao, Howard Chen, John Yang, Karthik Narasimhan
**Date:** 2022-07-04
**Primary category:** cs.CL
**All categories:** cs.CL, cs.AI, cs.LG

## Abstract
WebShop is one of the clearest early web-interaction environments with real products, human demonstrations, and both RL and imitation-learning baselines. It remains relevant as a foundational example of a task environment that is complex enough to teach strategy without requiring a full modern browser stack.
