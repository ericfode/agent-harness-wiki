# Viewpoints

**Source:** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1142/S0218194092000038)
**Authors:** A. Finkelstein, J. Kramer, B. Nuseibeh, L. Finkelstein, M. Goedicke
**Date:** 1992

## Core idea
The Viewpoints framework treats multiple perspectives as first-class and coordinates their integration rather than forcing them into one prematurely unified representation.

## Harness takeaway
`view_definition` objects in the studio should be legitimate viewpoints with explicit relations, scope, and crossings instead of temporary formatting choices.
