# SEVerA: Verified Synthesis of Self-Evolving Agents

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25111)
**Authors:** Debangshu Banerjee, Changming Xu, Gagandeep Singh
**Date:** 2026-03-26
**Primary category:** cs.LG
**All categories:** cs.LG, cs.PL, cs.SE

## Abstract
SEVerA treats self-evolving agent synthesis as a constrained learning problem with hard formal specifications and soft utility objectives. It is important because it shows a credible path to self-evolving agents that do not abandon correctness the moment they become ambitious.
