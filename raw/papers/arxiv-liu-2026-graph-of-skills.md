# Graph of Skills: Dependency-Aware Structural Retrieval for Massive Agent Skills

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05333)
**Authors:** Dawei Liu, Zongxia Li, Hongyang Du, Xiyang Wu, Shihang Gui, Yongbei Kuang, Lichao Sun
**Date:** 2026-04-07
**Primary category:** cs.AI
**All categories:** cs.AI

## Abstract
Graph of Skills constructs an executable dependency graph over large skill libraries and retrieves bounded, dependency-aware bundles at inference time. It is highly relevant once a system has many learned skills and needs retrieval to remain structural rather than merely semantic.
