# Training Software Engineering Agents and Verifiers with SWE-Gym

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.21139)
**Authors:** Jiayi Pan, Xingyao Wang, Graham Neubig, Navdeep Jaitly, Heng Ji, Alane Suhr, Yizhe Zhang
**Date:** 2024-12-30
**Primary category:** cs.SE
**All categories:** cs.SE, cs.CL

## Abstract
SWE-Gym provides executable codebases, tests, natural-language tasks, and trajectory data for training software-engineering agents and verifiers. It is especially important because it treats the verifier as a co-equal training object rather than as a decorative afterthought.
