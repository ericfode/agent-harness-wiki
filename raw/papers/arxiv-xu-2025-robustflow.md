# RobustFlow: Towards Robust Agentic Workflow Generation

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.21834)
**Authors:** Shengxiang Xu, Jiayi Zhang, Shimin Di, Yuyu Luo, Liang Yao, Hanmo Liu, Jia Zhu, Fan Liu, Min-Ling Zhang
**Date:** 2025-09-26
**Primary category:** cs.AI
**All categories:** cs.AI

## Abstract
RobustFlow focuses on a neglected but very practical issue: workflow generators often produce inconsistent workflows for semantically equivalent instructions. Its training objective explicitly teaches invariance to paraphrase and noise, which is useful if a control plane wants evolvable workflows without accidental churn.
