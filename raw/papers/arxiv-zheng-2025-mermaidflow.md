# MermaidFlow: Redefining Agentic Workflow Generation via Safety-Constrained Evolutionary Programming

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.22967)
**Authors:** Chengqi Zheng, Jianda Chen, Yueming Lyu, Wen Zheng Terence Ng, Haopeng Zhang, Yew-Soon Ong, Ivor Tsang, Haiyan Yin
**Date:** 2025-05-29
**Primary category:** cs.LG
**All categories:** cs.LG, cs.MA

## Abstract
MermaidFlow treats workflows as statically verifiable graph artifacts in Mermaid syntax and applies safety-constrained evolutionary operators over them. The paper matters because it joins workflow search with structural validity and interpretability rather than allowing the search space to collapse into malformed graph soup.
