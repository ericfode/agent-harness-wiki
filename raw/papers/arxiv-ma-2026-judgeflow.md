# JudgeFlow: Agentic Workflow Optimization via Block Judge

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.07477)
**Authors:** Zihan Ma, Zhikai Zhao, Chuanbo Hua, Federico Berto, Jinkyoo Park
**Date:** 2026-01-12
**Primary category:** cs.AI
**All categories:** cs.AI

## Abstract
JudgeFlow introduces a judge module that assigns responsibility scores to individual workflow blocks based on execution traces, especially failed runs. This is one of the cleanest papers on the missing middle between “we have a workflow” and “we know exactly which part deserves mutation.”
