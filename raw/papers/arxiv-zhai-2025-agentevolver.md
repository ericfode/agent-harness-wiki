# AgentEvolver: Towards Efficient Self-Evolving Agent System

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.10395)
**Authors:** Yunpeng Zhai, Shuchang Tao, Cheng Chen, Anni Zou, Ziqian Chen, Qingxu Fu, Shinji Mai, Li Yu, Jiaji Deng, Zouying Cao, Zhaoyang Liu, Bolin Ding, Jingren Zhou
**Date:** 2025-11-13
**Primary category:** cs.LG
**All categories:** cs.LG, cs.AI, cs.CL

## Abstract
AgentEvolver presents a broader self-evolving-agent framework built around self-questioning, self-navigation, and self-attribution. It is more about autonomous continual learning than workflow graphs specifically, but it is relevant as evidence that self-evolving-agent design is becoming a distinct literature rather than a slogan.
