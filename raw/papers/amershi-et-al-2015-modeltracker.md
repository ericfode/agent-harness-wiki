# ModelTracker

**Source:** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1145/2702123.2702509)
**Authors:** Saleema Amershi, Max Chickering, Steven M. Drucker, Bongshin Lee, Patrice Simard, Jina Suh
**Date:** 2015

## Core idea
ModelTracker links aggregate model summaries to example-level failures, letting practitioners move directly from a regression to the concrete cases that explain it.

## Harness takeaway
Counterfactual or model artifacts in the studio should bind metrics, summaries, and concrete failing cases into one inspectable object.
