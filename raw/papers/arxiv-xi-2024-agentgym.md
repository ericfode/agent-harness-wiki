# AgentGym: Evolving Large Language Model-based Agents across Diverse Environments

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.04151)
**Authors:** Zhiheng Xi, Yiwen Ding, Wenxiang Chen, Boyang Hong, Honglin Guo, Junzhe Wang, Dingwen Yang, Chenyang Liao, et al.
**Date:** 2024-06-06
**Primary category:** cs.AI
**All categories:** cs.AI, cs.CL

## Abstract
AgentGym is a broad multi-environment framework explicitly aimed at agent self-evolution rather than single-environment specialization. It is one of the papers most directly aligned with the question of RL-gym-like substrates for general harness improvement.
