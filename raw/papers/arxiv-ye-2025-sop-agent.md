# SOP-Agent: Empower General Purpose AI Agent with Domain-Specific SOPs

**Source:** [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.09316)
**Authors:** Anbang Ye, Qianran Ma, Jia Chen, Muqi Li, Tong Li, Fujiao Liu, Siqi Mai, Meichen Lu, Haitao Bao, Yang You
**Date:** 2025-01-16
**Primary category:** cs.AI
**All categories:** cs.AI

## Abstract
SOP-Agent represents domain SOPs as natural-language pseudocode and traversable decision graphs to guide task completion. It matters here because it makes operating procedure an explicit external object, which is often the first step before procedures themselves can evolve.
