# Provenance semirings

**Source:** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1145/1265530.1265535)
**Authors:** Todd J. Green, Grigoris Karvounarakis, Val Tannen
**Date:** 2007

## Core idea
Provenance semirings represent lineage algebraically so derived results preserve exactly which source facts or branches contributed to them.

## Harness takeaway
Branch merges, cherry-picks, evidence bundles, and derived artifacts in the studio should preserve explicit lineage expressions rather than flattening their ancestry.
