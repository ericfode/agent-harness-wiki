# Trigger.dev Product

**Source:** [Trigger.dev](https://www.trigger.dev/product)
**Topic:** Background jobs with realtime updates, observability, and control.

## Core idea
Trigger.dev presents durable background jobs with realtime updates, AI-agent support, observability, monitoring, concurrency controls, and queues.

## Key claims
- Long-running work should remain observable while it runs.
- Realtime updates belong in the product surface rather than being bolted on later.
- Background agents and queued work benefit from a dedicated control plane.

## Harness takeaway
This suggests a mission-control surface for autonomous coding work: live status, streams, approvals, concurrency controls, and the ability to intervene without collapsing the run.
