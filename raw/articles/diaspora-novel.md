# Diaspora

**Source:** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diaspora_(novel))
**Topic:** Divergent copies, long-latency federation, and lineage-preserving evolution.

## Core idea
Diaspora treats copied minds and long-delay communities as legitimate divergent branches with preserved lineage rather than as corruption of one central identity.

## Harness takeaway
Branch lineage and delayed federation should be first-class harness concepts. Divergence is not failure if provenance and merge semantics remain explicit.
