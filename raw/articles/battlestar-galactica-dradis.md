# DRADIS

**Source:** [Battlestar Wiki](https://en.battlestarwiki.org/DRADIS)
**Topic:** Central tactical picture with specialist filtering and uncertainty.

## Core idea
The CIC/DRADIS pattern combines a shared overview with specialist stations, clutter management, filtering, and false-positive handling.

## Harness takeaway
A harness should support shared overview plus role-specific panes, and should model uncertain or degraded sensing explicitly rather than pretending every signal is equally trustworthy.
