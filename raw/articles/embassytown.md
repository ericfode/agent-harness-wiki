# Embassytown

**Source:** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Embassytown)
**Topic:** Protocol semantics and ontology shifts as operational risk.

## Core idea
Embassytown dramatizes the fact that changing language or protocol semantics can destabilize the whole system, not just its syntax.

## Harness takeaway
Typed handoffs, protocol versioning, and ontology migrations in a harness should be treated as major control-plane events, not quiet prompt edits.
