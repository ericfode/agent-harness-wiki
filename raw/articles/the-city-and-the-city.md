# The City & The City

**Source:** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_City_%26_the_City)
**Topic:** Multiple valid views over one substrate with carefully governed crossings.

## Core idea
The City & The City is useful as a metaphor for multiple simultaneous realities rendered over one shared substrate, with boundary crossings treated as first-class events.

## Harness takeaway
A studio can use multiple synchronized `view_definition`s over one log, while treating cross-view leakage or bridge actions as auditable transitions rather than invisible magic.
