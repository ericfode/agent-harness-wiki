# Minority Report

**Source:** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minority_Report_(film))
**Topic:** Direct manipulation over evidence streams and predictive artifacts.

## Core idea
The memorable part is not the gloves. It is the treatment of evidence review as active arrangement, scrubbing, zooming, and composition across multiple feeds.

## Harness takeaway
A moldable operations studio should support direct manipulation over traces and evidence bundles rather than reducing review to passive log reading.
