# Machineries of Empire

**Source:** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machineries_of_Empire)
**Topic:** Shared protocol epochs and reality maintained through collective adherence.

## Core idea
The calendrical machinery in Machineries of Empire is a dramatic version of systems whose operational reality depends on a shared epoch and coordinated protocol adherence.

## Harness takeaway
A studio should track policy epochs, view epochs, and reconfiguration boundaries explicitly instead of hiding them behind silent settings drift.
