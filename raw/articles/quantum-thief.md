# The Quantum Thief

**Source:** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Quantum_Thief)
**Topic:** Partitioned exomemory and time as a spendable budget.

## Core idea
The Quantum Thief offers two relevant ideas: shared memory with access partitions, and operational life shaped by bounded time budgets.

## Harness takeaway
A studio can borrow both: labeled shared memory slices and escrow-like authority budgets that bound what agents may spend or do.
